About Our College

FG Degree College Hyderabad was established in 1969 as an educational initiative for people of Hyderabad to offer them quality education and opportunities for the future. For more than 70 years, FG Degree College has been providing quality education to the people of Hyderabad. FG College offers degree programs with intermediate education programs in a number of disciplines ranging from arts to science for the students of both Sindh Board and Federal Board. FG College is one of the few colleges in the Hyderabad Region providing Federal Board Education and is considered the most prestigious college in Hyderabad with the most affordable fee structures.

The Faculty, Administration, and Leadership of FG College is dedicated to providing the best possible educational and extracurricular activities to the students and maintaining the learning environment for its students.
